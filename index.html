01 Positioning

Bridging the Gap

"How similar or different do you think others' values are to yours?"

This is the unique research lens no other organisation is filling. It is where the most powerful insights for leaders emerge.

PCL occupies a distinctive space between academic values research — which often stays abstract and inaccessible — and commercial leadership consultancy — which often feels gimmicky or sales-driven.

We bridge that gap with hands-on leadership experience, rigorous data partnerships, and a founding belief that research insights should be freely available, not locked behind paywalls.

PCL is the megaphone for people's voices, not the judge of their values. We share what people are saying and open up questions.

02 Brand Persona Shorthand

What would Charlie Mackesy do?

Charlie connects to people's hearts with disarming simplicity. He makes the profound feel light. He meets complexity with warmth. He never performs, never oversells, and never takes an adversarial posture — even under pressure.

07 Tone of Voice

PCL Is

  • Open, attentive, eager to resolve.
  • Purposeful, intentional, aligned with the core.
  • Aligned with personal values, human, straight-talking.
  • Humble, evidence-focused, down-to-earth, proud but human.
  • Driven by connection, morals, and values.

PCL Is Not

  • Challenging, assuming it's right.
  • Criticising, conflictual.
  • Overselling, using buzzwords.
  • Business-like for its own sake, egocentric.
  • Adversarial, defensive, needing to win or explain itself.
